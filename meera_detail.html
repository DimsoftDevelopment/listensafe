Indian Actress

Meera Jasmine

"It's a privilege to support the ListenSafe campaign and the wonderful work the World Health Organisation are doing for Hearing loss and prevention around the World".
Meera Jasmine is an award winning Indian actress and entrepreneur who has made significant contributions to the realm of Indian cinema. With her extraordinary talent and captivating performances, she has established herself as a versatile and consummate actress across various regional languages, including Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada bagging laurels and accolades at regional and national levels across all realms of cinema.
Meera's exceptional contributions to Indian cinema have earned her numerous awards and accolades, including Filmfare Awards, State Film Awards, and National Film Awards. Her ability to bring authenticity and depth to her characters has resonated with audiences, making her a beloved and respected figure in the film industry.
Beyond her film career, Meera actively engages in philanthropic endeavors, supporting charitable causes and organizations dedicated to social betterment. Her artistic journey, marked by prodigious talent, versatility, and dedication, has solidified her status as an iconic and cherished figure in Indian cinema, leaving a lasting legacy for future generations to admire.
